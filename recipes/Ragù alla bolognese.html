Ragù alla bolognese


Ragù alla bolognese is a classic italian ground meat sauce that pairs beatifully with
pasta or lasagne. It's also a great dish for a cozy date evening, thanks to its comforting flavors.
It goes well with wine and the long cooking time
offers opportunities to bond with your partner :)



Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Tbsp parsley
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 350g ground meat
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Oregano
  • tomato pulp
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper



Needed kitchen-utensils

  • A vegetable knife
  • Large pot or pan
  • Cutting board
  • A sharp knife



Step by step

  1. Peel the onion and the garlic then cut it into small cubes. Wash and peel the carrots, cut the hard
    ends off the stalks, and chop or grate the rest very finely. Wash the parsley, shake dry and chop finely

  2. Heat the oil in a pot and sauté the garlic and onion for 4 minutes.
    Add the carrots and sauté for another 2 minutes.

  3. Now add the minced meat and fry briefly. Add tomato sauce, oregano and let the liquid reduce slightly.
    Put the lid on the pot and simmer over low heat for a good 40-50 minutes.

  4. Finally, stir in the tomato paste and season the sauce with salt, pepper and parsley.



Tips for the recipe

One secret ingredient is time, cook with low temperature!

Finishing with fresh herbs like basil or parsley, enhances the flavor and aroma

A high quality of ingredients also means high quality of food

Add salt and pepper at stages rather than all at once

Nothing speaks against a tiny little bit of fresh lemon juice
