Wiener Schnitzel


Wiener Schnitzel is an authenthic Austrian dish that
everyone has atleast tried, it's
made from breaded veal.



Ingredients

  • 160g veal
  • 4 pinches of salt
  • 150g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300g breadcrumbs
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 lemon



Needed kitchen-utensils

  • A cooking tenderizer (hammer)
  • Cling film
  • Pan or deep fryer



Step by step

  1. First, gently beat the schnitzels between cling film.
    Now salt the meat on both sides, turn it in flour, shake it
    off, dip it in the eggs and turn it in the breadcrumbs.

  2. Then bake the schnitzels about 2 fingers
    high in cooking oil until golden. Shake the pan a little while
    baking so that the schnitzels become evenly
    golden brown. Remove the schnitzel and drain on kitchen paper.

  3. Finally, cut the washed lemon into wedges and
    garnish the finished Wiener Schnitzel with lemon wedges.



Tips for the recipe

The classic Wiener Schnitzel is served with parsley potatoes or potato salad as a side dish

Be sure to use enough oil so that the schnitzel is baked floating.

The schnitzels should be fried immediately after breading so that the breading becomes crispy.
